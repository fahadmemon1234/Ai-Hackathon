# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the official guide for "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics"!

This book is designed to take you on a journey from the foundational principles of Physical AI to the exciting world of humanoid robotics. Whether you are a student, a hobbyist, or a professional, this guide will provide you with the knowledge and practical skills to build and program intelligent robots.

## What You Will Learn

- **Core Concepts of Physical AI**: Understand what it means for an AI to interact with the physical world.
- **Robot Operating System (ROS 2)**: Master the tools and concepts of ROS 2, the open-source standard for robotics.
- **Simulation Environments**: Learn to use Gazebo and NVIDIA Isaac Sim to test your robotic creations in virtual worlds.
- **Hardware and Electronics**: Get hands-on with the hardware required to build your own robots, with options for various budgets.
- **Advanced AI Integration**: Explore how to integrate cutting-edge AI models, like Visual Language Assistants (VLAs) and speech recognition, into your robotics projects.

## Getting Started

Ready to dive in? Navigate through the chapters using the sidebar to begin your learning adventure. We recommend starting with the "Foundational Concepts" section to build a strong base.
